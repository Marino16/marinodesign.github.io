Overview

Tiron is a desktop app that allows you to sync your calendars and keep track of your schedule in a single platform. Anyone can take book consultations in 3 simple clicks, whether they are free or paid.

Discover.

Requirements and desires
  • The request was to build an app that would allow users the booking of appointments and consultations in a few clicks, both from smartphones and laptop.
Approach

Once the design brief was analysed, I carried out an interview and research session to understand what were the business goals.

After comparing the strengths and weaknesses of our solutions with the team, I managed some research session to discover competitors and interview the client.
Then, I created the wireframes, carried out tests and finally created the final prototype.
