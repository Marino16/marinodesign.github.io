Overview

Tiron is a desktop app that allows you to sync your calendars and keep track of your schedule in a single platform. Anyone can take book consultations in 3 simple clicks, whether they are free or paid.

Discover.

The request
  • The need expressed was to have an app that would allow the booking of appointments and consultations in a few clicks.
Approach

Once the design brief was analysed, I carried out an interview and research session to understand what the competitors' core functionalities were.

After comparing the strengths and weaknesses of the other apps and interviewing the client, I created the wireframes, carried out tests and finally created the final prototype.
